The Daily Bruin and Bruinwalk are asking for your help.

The Daily Bruin and Bruinwalk.com Referendum calls for a $3 student fee increase to keep UCLA's daily student newspaper and student-run professor review site alive for years to come. For decades, we've relied on selling printed ads in the Daily Bruin as our main source of income. But now – as readers increasingly use our services online, and print advertising can't support us anymore – the Daily Bruin and Bruinwalk need your vote to let us continue serving the campus.
